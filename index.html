Research Theme

I investigate how how human activities and a warming climate shape the atmosphere’s oxidising capacity (OH/HO2, O3) and feed back on climate, specific weather phenomena (e.g., sea fog), and ecological health. I follow the full chain “Emission → Chemical Transformation → Weather / Climate Coupling → Atmospheric & Ecosystem Impacts”.

  • 🌇⛽️Emission Fingerprints : Quantify what and how much fossil‑energy extraction, petro‑chemical plants, shipping, and consumer products (VCPs) emit.
  • 🧊💥Chemical Transformation : Unpack how radicals and photochemistry turn those emissions into ozone and secondary pollutants; identify “missing sinks”.
  • 🌕💨Weather & Climate Coupling : Diagnose how heatwaves, boundary‑layer dynamics, sea‑breeze systems, etc. interact with chemistry and transport, altering pollutant loads.
  • 🌁🌲Atmospheric & Ecosystem Impacts : Link source–chemistry–weather interactions to climate feedbacks, specific weather events (e.g., sea fog), and ecosystem or health outcomes.
